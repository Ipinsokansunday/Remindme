About Us

RemindMe is dedicated to helping you keep track of all your important events. Our goal is to ensure you never miss a moment, whether it's a meeting, a birthday, or any other significant occasion. With our easy-to-use app, you can set reminders, customize alerts, and sync across all your devices.

Founded in 2024, we are a team of passionate individuals committed to improving the way you manage your time and events. We believe that with the right tools, you can stay organized and reduce the stress of forgetting important dates.
